2023!!! Here you are!! With great promises, the new year has arrived. Can't wait to see what you have in store for all of us. 2022, you have been eventful and challenging. Some of us had the most incredible year, while many people struggled at some point in time over the last year.

Even though Covid eased during the last year, we saw the dramatic effects of climate change, with record-breaking natural disasters in many parts of the world. Russia invaded Ukraine on 24th February 2022, resulting in thousands of deaths on both sides. Queen Elizabeth II, the longest-reigning British monarch, died. A twenty-two-year-old Kurdish-Iranian woman, Mahsa Amini, died in police custody after being arrested for failing to cover her hair. The humanitarian crisis in Syria, Afghanistan, South Sudan, and Yemen deepened.

Despite these challenges, we also saw some inspiring moments. After long years of oppression, more than 90000 Iranians marched as one to the slogan: "Women, life, freedom!". Countries agreed on climate and biodiversity funding. NASA launched the James Webb Space Telescope, giving us some of the most beautiful space pictures. The UK got its first Indian-origin PM, Elon Musk took over Twitter, and Taylor Swift released her tenth studio album. Above all, Lionel Messi took Argentina to the World Cup to lift it.

So it's true. 2022 has been incredible and hurtful. As we enter into the new year, let me assure you. This year isn't going to be a fairytale. There will be difficult times. It takes determination and aspiration to reach where you want to go. Many of us have made many tough decisions over the past year. We should all be proud of our efforts and support one another. Just take a moment to reflect on your experiences and accomplishments over the past twelve months. Let's use this opportunity to re-examine our values, goals and priorities.

It is the Holidays. Give rest to your body and soul. You owe to give them rest. Reflect on the inner you. Listen to its silent wishes. Do those silly crafts which make you feel content. Sing your heart out. Hop to the carol chorus. Enjoy the Christmas special matinee shows and make your own little straw hut for the savior, the salvage.

Want some inspiration to stay grounded even when life throws hardships in your face? Make sure that you watch The Terminal and The Shawshank Redemption. Jam to the tunes of Ain't No Mountain High Enough and Stronger (What Doesn't Kill You) and know that you can do anything if you put your heart into it.

Don't forget. Make 2023 the year you write your story - one of hope, resilience and progress.

Keep a lookout for NetX. Something colossal is coming soon.

Happy New Year!!!
