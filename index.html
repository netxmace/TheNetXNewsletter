“All the months go past
Each is like a guest
December is the last
December is the best
Each has lovely things
Each one is a friend
But December brings
Christmas is at its end!”

Hurray! The chimes of Big Ben rang!! It is Christmas!! Grab all your mischievous collections and hold them tight. I hope Papa Noel will not refuse to offer us a sledge ride.

The portly, jolly, white-bearded man in a red coat with a white fur collar and cuffs, red trousers, hat, black leather belt, and boots bringing a handful of candies to the well-behaved children, my Papa Noel never ceased to fascinate me.

Christmas induces joy and festivity in the family atmosphere. The aromas and the sounds of Christmas make the children rush home from school. Grandpa would put on Christmas carols, and Grandma would tap her feet to the beat as she mixed the batter, pounded dough, and fried, baked and molded treats for the family and all the neighbors. Dad and Mom would decorate the house in the evenings after work, and children would chant “Jingle bells, jingle bells!! Jingle all the way!!” until they faint. The air is cold and freezing. Anyone can feel the aroma of mindfully crafted plums and greeting cards. Messengers of love and peace are sent to the boundaries of the planet. Christ, the Redeemer, is born on my little straw hut.

With December, the Two Thousand Twenty-Two ( (TTTT)2022) dies. The love, affection, break-up, agony, frustration, fear, happiness and peace of 2022 will drown in the depths of Mariana.

It is the Holidays. Give rest to your body and soul. You owe to give them rest. Reflect on the inner you. Listen to its silent wishes. Do those silly crafts which make you feel content. Sing your heart out. Hop to the carol chorus. Enjoy the Christmas special matinee shows and make your own little straw hut for the savior, the salvage.

It is time for a Christmas resolution. I made mine. What about you? Still, waiting for the Ghost of Christmas past, present and future to come your way and lead your path..huh..? Waiting like Mr Scrooge for the day of compunction…? Why wait? No more waits!! Hop on to NetX. Let us change our destiny.

Do not forget to wish the gray guy next door a Merry Christmas.
