Hey you.

June's been busy and July looks like it's only about to get busier. But you know what I want most days in the morning, like on the one I'm typing this one out? Five extra minutes cozied up in my blanket. Or…you know…make that ten. Wait, no, 15….and so it goes.

Productivity, especially in this climate, is a hard thing to find the will for. Yes, the plans are elaborate, and hypothetically, everything looks doable. But in the grand calculus of the multiverse, well, plans hardly work. Now, I'm just being honest here. It's kind of a multiversal truth ya know. What does work, when all else fails, is improvisation. You make a plan, more like an outline, and you work out the kinks when we get to them. You improvise. Where am I going with this? Well, you'll see.

See improv is a skill, and not just a kind of stand up comedy. Thinking fast and acting on your feet is a skill we must all cultivate, because if something doesn't work in the first go, we may not always have the time to work on it right away. Notice that I said right away. There will always be second chances, just not in the way you imagine. Some of them might come years apart from the last one, and you might think missing them means the end of all of the chances at that particular thing. This is where we look for the famous plan B.

Where the improvisation comes in. But listen, even as we must learn to improvise and keep ironing the issues out, we mustn't forget that there will always be second chances. You just gotta watch out for them. So, even if you're not productive right this second, even if you want a little extra time wrapped up in a blanket, you can have it. Just make sure you eventually do get up. Kay?

Now, if you're looking for second, first or even third chances, and you don't fancy getting out of bed, we've brought a selection of chances right to you. Here you go! Think fast and grab your chances before they run out. Seize the day, my friend!
